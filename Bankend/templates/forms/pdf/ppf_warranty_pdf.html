Warranty Terms & Conditions

1. Inspection & Acceptance
The customer must inspect the vehicle at the time of delivery. Any concerns about the PPF installation must be raised immediately. Once the vehicle leaves the premises, it is considered accepted.

2. Curing Period
PPF requires 7-10 days to fully cure. During this period, minor haziness or small water bubbles may appear and will settle naturally. Customers are advised not to wash or wax the vehicle during this time.

3. Maintenance Responsibility
Regular washing and care are the customer's responsibility. Avoid using harsh chemicals or automatic car washes with brushes.

4. Warranty Coverage
Warranty applies to film defects such as yellowing, cracking, or peeling within the manufacturer's warranty period. It does not cover damage caused by accidents, misuse, or external factors like stone chips beyond film capacity.

5. After-Job Support
Customers may return for a free inspection within 7 days. Small adjustments will be corrected free of charge.

6. Exclusions
PPF is designed to protect from minor scratches but does not make the vehicle 100% damage-proof. Deep scratches, accidents, or improper maintenance are not covered. Warranty is non-transferable.

7. Film Removal
Removal must be carried out by our trained team. Any removal attempted by a third party may damage the paint and void the warranty.
